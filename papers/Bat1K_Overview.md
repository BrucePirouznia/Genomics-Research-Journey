# Bat1K Overview

## What is Bat1K?

Bat1K is a global scientific initiative aiming to sequence and assemble the genomes of all living bat species.

## Why is Bat1K Important?

Bats possess unique biological traits including exceptional longevity, resistance to many viruses, and specialized sensory adaptations.

## Research Relevance

Understanding bat genomes may provide insights into:

* Aging and longevity
* DNA repair mechanisms
* Immune system adaptations
* Evolutionary biology
